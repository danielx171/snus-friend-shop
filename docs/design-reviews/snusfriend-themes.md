## Theme A: "Midnight Teal" (Dark Premium)

### Color Tokens
```css
--bg-primary: #1A1A2E;
--bg-surface: #252538;
--bg-elevated: #2D2D44;
--text-primary: #F5F5F5;
--text-secondary: #A0A0B8;
--text-muted: #6B6B80;
--accent-primary: #0F6E56;
--accent-hover: #0D5C48;
--accent-glow: rgba(15, 110, 86, 0.15);
--border: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.08);
--border-hover: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.15);
```

### Typography
- **Headings:** Satoshi, 700-900 weight
- **Body:** General Sans, 400-500 weight
- **Tracking:** -0.02em headings, 0em body

### Vibe
Like a premium spirits website or high-end cocktail bar menu. Dark, confident, sophisticated. Product cans glow against the dark surface. Teal accents feel like neon reflections. The strength badges (green/yellow/orange/red) pop beautifully on dark backgrounds.

### Best For
- Product Detail Pages (hero product against dark canvas)
- Cart Drawer (dark overlay feels natural)
- Brand showcase pages
- Evening/night browsing experience

### Reference Sites
- velo.com (dark hero sections)
- deathwishcoffee.com (bold dark branding)
- ouraring.com (premium dark PDP)

## Theme B: "Clean Ritual" (Light Minimal)

### Color Tokens
```css
--bg-primary: #FAFAF8;
--bg-surface: #FFFFFF;
--bg-elevated: #F5F5F0;
--text-primary: #1A1A2E;
--text-secondary: #4A4A5A;
--text-muted: #8A8A9A;
--accent-primary: #0F6E56;
--accent-hover: #0D5C48;
--accent-light: rgba(15, 110, 86, 0.08);
--border: #E5E5E0;
--border-hover: #D0D0C8;
```

### Typography
- **Headings:** Cabinet Grotesk, 700-800 weight
- **Body:** Plus Jakarta Sans, 400-500 weight
- **Tracking:** -0.01em headings, 0.01em body

### Vibe
Like ritual.com but for pouches. Clean, trustworthy, medical-grade confidence without the clinical coldness. Generous whitespace lets products breathe. Teal accents feel like carefully placed garden stones. The off-white background (#FAFAF8) is warmer than pure white — subtle but critical for perceived quality.

### Best For
- Homepage (first impression = trust)
- Product Listing Pages (readability at scale)
- Category pages
- SEO landing pages (content-heavy)

### Reference Sites
- ritual.com (whitespace mastery)
- glossier.com (warm minimal)
- seed.com (editorial clean)
- allbirds.com (sustainable premium)

## Theme C: "Neon Pouch" (Bold Playful)

### Color Tokens
```css
--bg-primary: #0A0A0A;
--bg-surface: #141414;
--bg-elevated: #1E1E1E;
--text-primary: #FFFFFF;
--text-secondary: #B0B0B0;
--text-muted: #707070;
--accent-primary: #00E5A0; /* bright teal-green */
--accent-secondary: #FF6B6B; /* coral red */
--accent-tertiary: #7C5CFF; /* electric purple */
--border: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.06);
--border-hover: rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.12);
```

### Typography
- **Headings:** Outfit, 700-800 weight
- **Body:** Switzer, 400-500 weight
- **Tracking:** -0.03em headings (tight, bold), 0em body

### Vibe
Like Recess/Olipop energy but edgier. Pure black canvas with vibrant per-flavor accent colors that shift as you browse. Product cards have colored border glows matching the flavor family. Hover effects pulse. Navigation uses rounded pill shapes. The strength badges are neon-bright against the void. Feels like a premium vape shop or sneaker store — youthful, confident, unapologetic.

### Best For
- Brand pages (each brand gets its own color accent)
- Promotional/seasonal pages
- Social media landing pages
- Younger demographic targeting

### Reference Sites
- takearecess.com (color shifts, personality)
- drinkolipop.com (bold flavor-per-color)
- xqs.com (lifestyle brand energy)

## Theme Comparison Matrix

| Attribute | A: Midnight Teal | B: Clean Ritual | C: Neon Pouch |
|-----------|------------------|-----------------|---------------|
| Background | Dark (#1A1A2E) | Light (#FAFAF8) | Black (#0A0A0A) |
| Feel | Sophisticated | Trustworthy | Bold |
| Product pop | High (dark canvas) | Medium (white clean) | Very high (neon glow) |
| Readability | Good (with contrast) | Excellent | Good (bright on dark) |
| Trust signal | Premium exclusivity | Medical confidence | Youth energy |
| Best page | PDP, Cart | Homepage, PLP | Brand, Promo |
| Risk | Can feel heavy | Can feel generic | Can feel gimmicky |
| Target age | 25-45 | 28-50 | 18-30 |

## Recommended Approach: Hybrid

Don't pick one theme — use all three strategically:

1. **Theme B** for homepage, PLPs, category pages (first impression = trust)
2. **Theme A** for header, footer, PDP hero, cart drawer (premium contrast)
3. **Theme C** elements for brand pages, promotions, hover states (energy + personality)

This creates visual rhythm: light → dark → light → dark as users scroll through sections, preventing the visual monotony that plagues single-theme sites.
