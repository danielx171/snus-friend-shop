Design Notes — Zen PDP (M)

Philosophy: Scandinavian clean — off-white (#FAFAF8) background, forest green (#1B4332) accent, maximum whitespace. The product and information hierarchy do the selling, not visual noise. Accordion for secondary content (ingredients, reviews, shipping) keeps above-the-fold clean. Strength pills use pastel backgrounds that feel gentle but remain distinctly color-coded.

Why this scores 8.80: All 5 signature UX patterns present · Accessibility-first color contrast on light background · Generous touch targets on selectors · Clean hierarchy guides the eye: Brand → Name → Rating → Price → Strength → Pack → Subscribe → Cart. Cross-sell has flavor-coded borders. Trust bar sits at the natural "decision point" right above the fold break.

🏆 Final Recommendations — The SnusFriend Design System

Option A: "Scandinavian Premium" (recommended) — Light base (Scandi Forest #FAFAF8 + #1B4332) for homepage, PLP, and PDP. Use Design M (Zen PDP, 8.80) + Design J (Mobile HP, 8.73) + Design F (Light PLP). Cart drawer stays dark (R1 design, 8.45). Checkout uses Copper Glass (Design I, 8.93). This gives you the best accessibility scores, fastest perceived performance, and a clean editorial feel that builds trust.

Option B: "Dark Connoisseur" — Dark base throughout. Use Design K (Hybrid PDP, 9.18) + Design L (Vault HP, 8.65) for a premium luxury feel. Copper accent throughout checkout. Higher visual impact but needs careful accessibility auditing.

Option C: "Split Personality" — Light homepage + PLP (trust-building, scan-friendly), dark PDP + cart + checkout (immersive, conversion-optimized). Best of both worlds — visitors browse in light mode, then enter a "focused buying zone" when they commit to a product.
