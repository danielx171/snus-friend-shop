## Release 2: Earn Rewards While You Shop

**Version:** v1.4.0
**Date:** 21 March 2026

We've added a whole community layer to Snusfriends. Complete quests, earn points for every purchase, climb the leaderboard, and unlock achievements. Plus, share your honest reviews of products — other customers will see which pouches are best.

**What you get:**
- Customise your profile with a unique avatar and display name
- Leave product reviews and see which items have the best feedback
- Complete daily and weekly quests to rack up points
- Earn achievement badges (bronze, silver, gold, diamond) as you shop more
- Redeem your points for discounts and exclusive rewards
- See what other shoppers are up to on the live activity feed
- Advanced search that actually understands your preferences

**Try it now:** Create your profile and [check out the quest board](/quests) to start earning.

## Optional Additional Notes for Editorial

**Tone considerations:**
- Use conversational language — imagine explaining these features to a mate
- Focus on the benefit first, tech details second (or skip entirely)
- Use "you" and "your" to speak directly to the customer
- Avoid version numbers unless necessary; customers don't care that it's v1.3.0

**CTA suggestions:**
- Add "Try it now" links pointing to the relevant product/feature page
- Consider a final CTA: "Questions? We're here to help — email support@snusfriends.com"
- Feature request CTA at the end works well (keep the existing copy)

**Timeline display:**
- Keep the vertical timeline design — it's visually appealing
- Use the friendly descriptions in the timeline cards instead of bullet points
- The bullet points work well as sub-items under the description

**SEO metadata:**
