## Campaign Guidelines

**Sending Schedule:**
- Week 1: Send targets 1, 5, 8 (high authority, policy, industry)
- Week 2: Send targets 2, 4, 9 (commercial partners, direct competitors)
- Week 3: Send targets 3, 7, 11 (content sites, health orgs)
- Week 4: Send targets 6, 10, 13 (data partners, directory sites)
- Week 5: Send targets 12, 14, 15 (niche communities, review sites)

**Follow-up Strategy:**
- Follow-up 1: 7-10 days. Keep it short—reference the original email, ask if it reached the right person.
- Follow-up 2: 14-21 days. Offer a specific sweetener (data export, guest post, exclusive content).
- Do not send more than 2 follow-ups per target.

**Success Metrics:**
- Target: 5-7 backlinks from this list (33–47% conversion rate is realistic for personalized outreach)
- Preferred anchors: "nicotine pouch brands," "strength chart," "EU legal guide," "flavor database"
- Monitor in GSC for index status and traffic impact.

**Red Flags / Do Not Send:**
- Sites that explicitly forbid guest posts or link exchanges
- Sites with obvious comment spam or low domain authority (<20 DA)
- Sites that require paid link placement or "sponsored content"
- Private Facebook groups or closed communities (no outreach email)
