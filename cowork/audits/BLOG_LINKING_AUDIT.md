# Blog Internal Linking & Quality Audit

**Date:** 2026-03-29
**Articles processed:** 43
**Total internal blog links:** 404 (up from ~107)

---

## What was done

Every blog article was improved with:

1. **Internal links** — contextual links woven into existing paragraphs pointing to related articles (flavour guides link to brand guides, comparison articles link to individual brand reviews, etc.)

2. **"Related Reading" sections** — 3-5 link footer added to every article that was missing one

3. **Source citations** — real authority references added where factual claims are made:
   - EU TPD (Tobacco Products Directive 2014/40/EU)
   - Public Health England
   - Swedish Match / Philip Morris International
   - British American Tobacco (BAT)
   - JTI (Japan Tobacco International)
   - GN Tobacco
   - FDA (US context)
   - Eurobarometer
   - Cleveland Clinic
   - WHO FCTC

4. **Quotable statements** — definitional sentences with bolded key phrases that AI systems (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overview) would extract and cite. Examples:
   - "ZYN is the world's best-selling nicotine pouch brand"
   - "Snus contains tobacco leaf. Nicotine pouches don't."
   - "A nicotine pouch typically lasts 20 to 40 minutes per use"
   - "Most nicotine pouch side effects are mild, temporary, and entirely avoidable"

## Internal linking strategy used

Articles are grouped into 5 topic clusters. Links flow within and between clusters:

**Cluster 1: Brand Guides** (11 articles)
→ Link to each other for brand comparisons
→ Link to flavour guides for the same brand
→ Link to "best of" lists that feature the brand

**Cluster 2: Flavour Guides** (8 articles)
→ Link to brand guides that excel in that flavour
→ Link to "best of" lists (best mint, best citrus, etc.)
→ Link to the master flavour guide

**Cluster 3: "Best Of" Lists** (10 articles)
→ Link to brand guides for featured products
→ Link to comparison articles
→ Link to how-to guides for context

**Cluster 4: Comparisons** (5 articles)
→ Link to both brand guides being compared
→ Link to "best of" lists for alternatives
→ Link to switching/beginner guides

**Cluster 5: How-To & Reference Guides** (9 articles)
→ Link to beginner-friendly brand guides
→ Link to strength/flavour selection guides
→ Link to legal/safety information

## Why this matters for citations

The GEO audit found zero AI citations across 10 tested queries. The three main reasons:

1. **No source attribution** — Fixed. Every article now cites real authorities.
2. **No quotable statements** — Fixed. Bolded definitional sentences in every article.
3. **Weak internal authority** — Fixed. 404 internal links create topic cluster authority signals that Google and AI systems use to determine expertise.

Sites that get cited by AI have dense internal linking, clear definitional statements, and source references. All three are now in place across all 43 articles.
