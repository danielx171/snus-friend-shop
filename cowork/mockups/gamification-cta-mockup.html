Gamification Visibility Mockups

Three approaches to make the gamification system visible to visitors without being intrusive.

Option A: Floating Spin-to-Win Button (Bottom-Right)

A subtle floating button that pulses gently, drawing attention without blocking content. Expands on hover to show the reward. Sits above the cookie banner.

🎡 Spin & Win! Free pouches

Option B: Header Points Indicator

A compact points display in the header next to the cart icon. Shows points balance for logged-in users, or "Join & Earn" for guests. Links to /rewards.

Recommendation: Use Option A + B together. The floating CTA catches scrollers, the header badge retains logged-in users.

Recommended Approach

Combine A + B: The floating Spin-to-Win button (Option A) drives first engagement — it's visible to every visitor regardless of page. The header points indicator (Option B) keeps returning users engaged with their balance visible at all times. Option C can be added to the homepage as a secondary enhancement. The floating button should dismiss after the user interacts with it (either spins or explicitly closes), and shouldn't reappear for 24 hours.
