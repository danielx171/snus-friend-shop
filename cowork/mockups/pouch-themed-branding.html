Pouch-Themed Design Elements

Design vocabulary built from the physical product: tin lids, pouch shapes, strength dots, and the diamond-leaf logo mark.

Strength Meter Bars DO IT

Product cards, PDP, filter chips
Replace the text-only strength labels with a visual meter of ascending bars — like the signal strength icon on a phone but themed to nicotine. Instantly scannable.
