ZYN Flavour Categories

ZYN's flavour portfolio spans seven distinct categories. Each reflects different taste profiles, cultural preferences, and user habits. Understanding these categories helps you narrow down your options quickly.

Mint & Menthol

Mint is ZYN's largest and most popular flavour family, accounting for roughly 40% of sales globally. These pouches deliver clean, cool sensations ranging from soft spearmint to intense menthol ice. Mint variants work year-round but are especially popular during warmer months.

Within this category, Spearmint offers a gentle, naturally sweet minty experience—ideal for newcomers or those who find regular mint too strong. Cool Mint takes a step up with a crisper, more pronounced flavour and slightly higher strengths. Gentle Mint splits the difference: less intense than Cool Mint but more pronounced than Spearmint.

Menthol Ice and Cool Mint Ice represent the extreme end, delivering a frozen, mouth-numbing intensity beloved by experienced users. Mint Fizz adds a subtle carbonated twist, creating a fizzy-mint hybrid that stands alone in the range. Cactus Spice rounds out the mint family with an unexpected desert-herb note—a newer introduction that's gaining traction among adventurous users.

Available strengths: 1.5 mg (Spearmint Mini only), 3 mg, 6 mg, 6.5 mg, 9 mg, 10.6 mg, 11 mg, 13.5 mg, 16.5 mg

Best for: Mint lovers, daytime users, those transitioning from cigarettes, menthol smokers

Citrus

Citrus flavours bring brightness and energy to the ZYN range. Where mint dominates calm and focus, citrus energises and refreshes. The category is smaller than mint but growing, with 5 distinct products.

Citrus is the workhorse here—a balanced blend of lemon, lime, and orange that doesn't overwhelm. It sits naturally between sweet and tart, making it accessible to most palates. Lemon Spritz, ZYN's newer addition, leans harder into fresh lemon with a subtle fizz, directly competing with citrus energy drinks for the user's attention.

Available strengths: 3 mg, 6 mg, 9 mg, 11 mg

Best for: Morning users, gym-goers, those seeking mental clarity, users who dislike mint

Berry & Red Fruits

Berry flavours appeal to users seeking something sweeter without being confectionery-like. This category blends red berry, blackcurrant, and dark fruit notes into a cohesive range that feels autumn-like and slightly sophisticated.

Red Berry Fizz combines berry sweetness with a light effervescence, creating a candy-like but not overwhelming experience. Red Fruits Fizz takes a wider fruit palette—mixing strawberry, raspberry, and blueberry—and applies the same fizz treatment. Blackcurrant Ice, the strongest berry option, delivers a dark, tart blackcurrant with a cool menthol undertone, making it ideal for cold-weather use.

Available strengths: 9 mg, 11 mg, 13.5 mg

Best for: Sweet-tooth users, afternoon breaks, those avoiding mint, female users (historically higher uptake in this segment)

Coffee

Coffee flavours target users who associate nicotine with their morning ritual—and for whom the pairing with an actual cup of coffee makes intuitive sense. ZYN's coffee range is modest but well-executed, spanning from approachable Coffee to bold Dark Roast.

Coffee Mini delivers a smooth, roasted flavour at low strengths, perfect for those just starting out. Macchiato Slim introduces espresso with a hint of cream, creating a more complex, cafe-like experience. Dark Roast Slim strips back the cream and amps up the intensity, landing as ZYN's strongest coffee variant.

Available strengths: 3 mg, 6 mg, 9 mg, 11 mg

Best for: Morning routine anchors, coffee drinkers, office workers, those seeking familiarity

Fruit

The Fruit category casts a wider net than Berry & Red Fruits, including stone fruits (peach, cherry), tropical notes (apple mint), and vegetables-as-fruit (cucumber). This category offers the most diversity in single-flavour products.

Peach Mini is soft and summery, while Black Cherry offers something darker and more sophisticated. Apple Mint bridges mint and fruit categories, making it ideal for users who want menthol's cool sensation with fruity sweetness. Cucumber Lime Slim leans into refreshment, almost spa-like in its clarity. Red Fruits Slim shares DNA with the Berry category but sits here as a distinct entry point.

Available strengths: 3 mg, 6 mg, 9 mg, 11 mg

Best for: Adventurous users, summer season, those seeking light, clean flavours, users avoiding mint dominance

Licorice

Licorice is ZYN's most polarising category—beloved in Scandinavia, where liquorice sweets and sauces are cultural mainstays, and more niche elsewhere. These pouches deliver authentic anise-forward flavours, not candy-like sweetness.

Black Licorice is the classic, offering pure anise root in pouch form. Mini Dry Black Licorice removes moisture for a more concentrated, drier experience. Violet Licorice adds a floral herbal twist, making it slightly more approachable than straight licorice.

Available strengths: 3 mg, 6 mg, 9 mg

Best for: Nordic users, licorice enthusiasts, those seeking something truly different, users with adventurous palates

Original

Original is ZYN's plain-spoken entry point—a neutral, slightly sweet base with minimal flavour interference. It's ideal for users who want the nicotine experience without sensory distraction, or who are still discovering their preferences.

Available strengths: 3 mg, 6 mg

Best for: First-time users, those testing their tolerance, users who prefer subtle flavours

ZYN Flavours by Strength

ZYN's strength scale runs from 1.5 mg (entry-level) to 16.5 mg (maximum). Strengths are designated by size format and power level:

Strength Level Nicotine Content Format Ideal For
S1 / Entry 1.5–3 mg Mini pouches First-time users, testing preferences, light daytime use
S2 / Light 3–6 mg Mini or Slim Beginners, frequent dosing (multiple pouches daily), casual users
S3 / Regular 6–9 mg Slim Moderate users, social use, transitioning from cigarettes
S4 / Strong 10.6–11 mg Slim Regular users, former smokers, all-day use
S5 / Very Strong 13.5 mg Slim Experienced users, high tolerance, demanding situations
S6 / Maximum 16.5 mg Slim Power users, extreme tolerance, special occasions (e.g., festivals)

Most users find their home at S3–S4 (6–11 mg). If you're new to nicotine pouches, start at S1–S2 and increase only if you find yourself craving more frequent use. If you're transitioning from cigarettes, S3–S4 typically mirrors a standard cigarette's nicotine yield.

How to Choose Your ZYN Flavour

Step 1: Identify Your Baseline Taste. Do you like mint, fruit, coffee, or something neutral? If you've used snus, cigarettes, or other nicotine products, your preference probably hasn't changed. If you're new to nicotine, consider what you enjoy in food and drink: do you reach for mints, candy, coffee, or citrus drinks?

Step 2: Assess Your Strength Needs. Strength is personal and depends on your background. Former cigarette smokers typically need S3–S4. First-time nicotine users should start at S1–S2. If you're a seasoned snus user, S4–S6 may feel right. Don't confuse strength with satisfaction—a weaker flavour you genuinely enjoy beats a strong one you tolerate.

Step 3: Consider Your Use Case. Are you using ZYN for focus during work? Relaxation after meals? Social occasions? Mint works best for alertness; fruit and coffee for ritual moments; licorice for sensory novelty. Strength also matters here: S6 for occasional power use; S3–S4 for daily habit.

Step 4: Sample Before Committing. Buy a single can (or a few pouches if your local vendor allows loose sales) before committing to a multi-can order. Flavour preference is highly individual, and what's beloved by thousands might not suit you. SnusFriend offers a flavour quiz to guide initial choices.

Step 5: Track and Refine. After a week, assess: Did you enjoy the flavour? Did the strength feel right? Did you crave more or less frequent use? Adjust from there. Most users land on 2–3 go-to flavours and cycle between them.
