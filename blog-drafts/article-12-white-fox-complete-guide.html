Who Is White Fox?

White Fox is the thinking person's strong-strength brand. Made by GN Tobacco (the same Swedish company behind Siberia), White Fox launched in 2019 to fill a gap between mainstream moderates (Loop, Velo) and the ultra-extreme (Siberia). It's a brand that says: "You know what you want. You want mint. You want strength. Nothing else."

No gimmicks. No limited editions. No switching flavours. Just consistent, refined mint at nicotine levels that appeal to serious users who've outgrown light-strength brands.

Flavour Profiles — Detailed

All White Portion (12 mg) — The Entry Gateway

Don't let "All White" fool you. This is pure, clean mint without artificial sweetness or cooling gimmicks. It's the flavour equivalent of a well-tailored suit: understated, elegant, no fuss. If you want to taste White Fox without commitment, this is the introduction.

Best for: Daily rotation, all-day use, users transitioning from lighter brands.

Double Mint (12 mg) — The Bolder Cousin

Same strength as All White Portion, but the mint is amplified. Some users report subtle spice undertones — not "flavoured" in the artificial sense, but the result of concentrated mint essence. It's still mint-only; just more of it per pouch.

Best for: Mint enthusiasts who want intensity without climbing to 14+ mg.

Peppered Mint (14.3 mg) — The Sophisticated Deviation

White Fox's only non-neutral entry. "Peppered" doesn't mean chilli or spice-blend; it's warm, slightly peppery botanical notes layered under the mint. It's White Fox's way of saying, "You can have complexity without abandoning purity." Some compare it to spearmint with warmth.

Best for: Users bored by neutral mint but not ready for full-strength extremes; palette adventurers within the mint family.

Full Charge (16.5 mg) — The Sweet Spot

This is where White Fox becomes dangerous in the best way. At 16.5 mg, you get serious nicotine satisfaction without the cult ultra-strength persona. The mint is sharp, clean, and unapologetic. Full Charge is the pouch most daily users keep rotating. It's strong enough to matter, balanced enough for repeat use.

Best for: Heavy users, ex-smokers, all-day professionals, anyone comfortable with high-strength but not chasing extreme limits.

Black Edition (24 mg) — Maximum Intent

White Fox's ceiling. At 24 mg, this is a specialist pouch for users who've already proven they handle 16+ mg well. The mint is intense, the nicotine hit is serious, and the package is slim — maximum punch in minimal volume. It's not for habit rotation; it's for when you need the ultimate mint experience.

Best for: Extreme-strength seekers who prefer White Fox's mint refinement over Siberia's cooling theatre; competitors to brands like Siberia Ultra Slim (16.5 mg) and above.

The White Fox Strength Ladder

  • Entry (12 mg): All White Portion, Double Mint — approachable strong.
  • Mid (14.3 mg): Peppered Mint — stepping stone to 16+.
  • High (16.5 mg): Full Charge — the practical maximum for daily users.
  • Extreme (24 mg): Black Edition — ultra-specialist territory.

Unlike brands offering 6–11.5 mg ranges, White Fox assumes you're already at 12+. There's no "beginner tier." This is by design.
