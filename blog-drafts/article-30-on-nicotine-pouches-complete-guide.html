Introduction: Who is ON! and Why Should You Care?

ON! is the American nicotine pouch brand owned by Altria, one of the world's largest tobacco and nicotine companies. While ON! originated in the US market, it has expanded rapidly into Europe since 2023, capitalising on strong distribution networks and investment in regulatory compliance.

For European consumers, ON! represents a compelling alternative to established brands like VELO, ZYN, and Nordic Spirit. ON! offers the best cost-per-mg value among major European brands, proprietary nicotine technology (NICOSILK) exclusive to ON! PLUS, and a flavour portfolio designed for both beginners and experienced users.

This guide covers everything you need to know about ON! nicotine pouches: the full product lineup, detailed flavour tasting notes, strength recommendations by user type, NICOSILK technology explained, and how ON! compares to competitors.

ON! Brand Overview and History

ON! launched in the US in 2015 as Altria's direct competitor to competitors in the emerging nicotine pouch category. Altria, best known for Marlboro cigarettes and Copenhagen snus, leveraged its vast resources to develop and market ON! as a premium, tobacco-free alternative.

Key milestones:

  • 2015–2017: US market launch and rapid scaling; ON! becomes #2 brand by market share in North America.
  • 2018–2022: Regulatory challenges in US; expansion into Canada and test markets in Europe and Japan.
  • 2023–2024: EU expansion accelerates under TPD3 compliance; ON! establishes German distribution hub and enters UK, Benelux, and Scandinavian markets.
  • 2025–2026: ON! is now one of the top 5 nicotine pouch brands in Europe by volume; pricing strategy favours aggressive market penetration over margin maximisation.

Why Altria chose aggressive European expansion: Regulated markets (EU, UK) offer stability and higher profit margins than developing markets. Altria's cigarette volumes are declining in developed regions; nicotine pouches are seen as a higher-margin replacement. ON! is the flagship product for this strategy.

ON! Product Lineup: Classic vs PLUS

ON! Classic is the core product line, using traditional plant-fibre pouches with standard nicotine delivery. Designed for beginners and users who prefer gradual nicotine absorption.

ON! PLUS is the premium tier, featuring NICOSILK technology (explained below) for 30% faster nicotine delivery. Designed for ex-smokers and experienced users seeking rapid, satisfying nicotine hits.

Product specification comparison:

  • Pouch size: Both Classic and PLUS use slim white pouches (~0.35 g)
  • Pouch material: Classic uses plant-based fibre; PLUS uses enhanced fibre with silk-protein microcapsules
  • Available strengths: Both: 2 mg, 4 mg, 6 mg, 8 mg (some regions may have limited variants)
  • Flavour overlap: Core flavours (Mint, Coffee, Citrus, Cinnamon, Berry) are available in both Classic and PLUS
  • Price difference: ON! PLUS typically costs €0.15–€0.25 more per can than Classic due to NICOSILK technology

Recommended use cases:

  • Start with ON! Classic: You're completely new to nicotine pouches and want to assess tolerance gradually
  • Start with ON! PLUS: You're an ex-smoker and want nicotine delivery speed similar to smoking; you already use other nicotine pouches and want a faster variant

NICOSILK Technology Explained

What is NICOSILK?

NICOSILK is Altria's proprietary nicotine delivery system exclusive to ON! PLUS. It uses silk-protein microcapsules embedded in the pouch fibre that dissolve in saliva, releasing nicotine 30% faster than traditional fibre pouches.

How it works (simplified):

  • Traditional pouches: Nicotine is dissolved uniformly in the fibre matrix; absorption is gradual and temperature-dependent
  • NICOSILK pouches: Nicotine is contained in microscopic silk-protein capsules that rupture on contact with saliva, creating a controlled-release burst

User-facing benefits:

  • Faster onset: Peak nicotine in 5–8 minutes (vs 8–12 minutes for Classic). Mimics smoking's rapid dopamine spike.
  • Smoother throat hit: Rapid delivery is accompanied by a gentler throat sensation compared to snus due to silk-protein's smoother dissolution
  • Sustained satisfaction: Users report longer-lasting satisfaction (60–90 min) due to the controlled-release profile
  • Reduced nausea risk: Despite rapid delivery, silk-protein's smooth release profile results in fewer reports of nicotine-induced nausea compared to other rapid-delivery systems

Cost trade-off: NICOSILK technology adds approximately €0.15–€0.25 per can (~5–10% premium), but many ex-smokers consider the faster, smoother delivery worth the premium.

Is NICOSILK worth the extra cost? Yes, if you're an ex-smoker seeking rapid nicotine delivery with minimal throat irritation. No, if you're new to nicotine pouches or prefer gradual absorption.
