How to Choose Your Strength

Nicotine strength is measured in milligrams and indicates how much nicotine you'll absorb per pouch. There's no "right" strength — only what works for you.

  • 0–3 mg (Ultra-light): Barely noticeable effect; best for curiosity or habit-building without stimulation
  • 4–6 mg (Light to Medium): Subtle but perceptible nicotine buzz; ideal for new users or those preferring restraint
  • 6–8 mg (Medium to Strong): Clear, noticeable effect; suits regular users and those accustomed to nicotine
  • 8+ mg (Strong): Intense effects; for experienced users with high nicotine tolerance

If you're new to nicotine pouches, starting with 4–5 mg allows you to gauge your tolerance without overwhelming yourself. You can always move up or down depending on how you feel.

Tips for Using Nicotine Pouches

  • Place behind your upper lip. Position the pouch between your gum and upper lip — not between your teeth, which can break the pouch.
  • Start with 20–30 minutes. Each pouch lasts 45–60 minutes on average, but begin conservatively and extend if you enjoy it.
  • Don't swallow the juice. Nicotine pouches release flavoured liquid; spit it out if you accidentally swallow saliva, or simply let it drip naturally into your mouth.
  • Store in a cool place. Pouches stay fresh at room temperature and don't require refrigeration like snus.
  • Try multiple strengths and flavours. Your preference may surprise you; don't assume what you'll like based on other nicotine products.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are nicotine pouches safe?
Nicotine pouches are less harmful than cigarettes because they don't involve burning or inhalation of smoke. However, nicotine itself is addictive and not risk-free — it raises heart rate and blood pressure. If you have cardiovascular concerns, are pregnant, or take medications that interact with nicotine, consult a healthcare provider. Nicotine-free alternatives like Zeronito or VELO Zero offer the habit without the stimulant.
How long does a nicotine pouch last?
Most standard pouches last 45–60 minutes. Mini pouches typically last 20–30 minutes due to their smaller size. The exact duration depends on your saliva flow and how much you move the pouch around. Stronger pouches can feel "spent" slightly faster because the nicotine is absorbed more quickly.
Will nicotine pouches stain my teeth?
Unlike snus or tobacco products, nicotine pouches contain no tobacco and won't stain your teeth. They're white, and the pouches remain in your mouth without direct contact with your tooth surface. Excellent oral hygiene is always recommended, but pouches are gentler on teeth than traditional nicotine products.
Can I use nicotine pouches if I'm new to nicotine?
Yes, but start low. Begin with a 4–5 mg pouch and use for 20–30 minutes to gauge your tolerance. Nicotine can cause dizziness, nausea, or headaches if you're unaccustomed to it — these effects usually subside as your body adapts. What's the difference between slim and mini pouches?

Slim pouches are 45–60 minutes. If maximum discretion is your priority, mini is best; if you prefer longer sessions and comfort, slim is the better choice.
