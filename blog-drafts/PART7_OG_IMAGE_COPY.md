## Design Notes

### Tagline Strategy
- **Action-led:** "Go smoke-free", "Read deeper", "Pouches win" — verbs first
- **Uniqueness:** Each tagline avoids repetition; tailored to page intent
- **Social clip:** Taglines work at 280px width (mobile feed standard)
- **Brand voice:** Authoritative without jargon; British understatement

### Subtitle Strategy
- **Value first:** Lead with what the reader gets (731 products, 200+ flavours, ranked data)
- **Proof:** Numbers signal authority ("50+ tested", "29+ brands", "43 articles")
- **Conversational:** No marketing clichés; speaks like a trusted friend
- **15-word target:** Readable in one glance; scannable on mobile

### Background Mood
- **Forest base:** Aligns with primary brand colour (#1a4d2e)
- **Variation:** Each page gets a distinct mood to avoid visual fatigue in feed
- **Texture hints:** Leaf motif (homepage), mint (ZYN), spice (VELO), split-screen (vaping comparison)
- **Contrast:** Blog pages use warmer/editorial feels; product pages more premium/cool

### Implementation
1. **Social platforms:** Use tagline + subtitle as default OG text; include brand mark
2. **Search preview:** Google SERP will show tagline + 120 chars of subtitle
3. **Email campaigns:** Taglines work as subject line hooks; subtitles as preview text
4. **Print/content:** Adapt for posters, newsletters, social cards
