## PART 6: COMMON PITFALLS & HOW TO AVOID THEM

| **Pitfall** | **Risk** | **Prevention** |
|---|---|---|
| **Pitching before content is published** | Low response rate; looks unfinished | Always publish first, then pitch + send proof |
| **Generic "link to us" emails** | 90%+ deletion rate | Personalize with specific reference to their recent article |
| **Boring research claims** | No news value; no media coverage | Frame as "first European study on [topic]"; tie to news peg |
| **Targeting low-DA sites only** | Minimal SEO value; looks spammy | Mix Tier 1 (65+ DA) + Tier 2 (40–65 DA) outreach |
| **Not following up** | ~30% of responses come after 2nd email | Send 2–3 follow-up emails over 4 weeks |
| **Offering "exclusive" content everywhere** | Dilutes exclusivity | Reserve truly exclusive offers for Tier 1 (5–10 sites) |
