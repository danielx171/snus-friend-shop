# LOOP vs Skruf: Which Swedish Brand Is Right for You?

**Meta description:** A detailed comparison of LOOP and Skruf nicotine pouches—flavor range, strength, pouch format, and which brand suits different users.

## Introduction

If you're shopping for nicotine pouches, you've probably seen LOOP and Skruf everywhere. Both are Swedish, both have strong followings, and both make genuinely good products. But they're actually quite different in philosophy. One is the experimental upstart chasing wild flavor combinations; the other is the established player focused on refinement and consistency. This comparison will help you figure out which one actually matches how you use pouches and what matters to you in your nicotine experience.

## LOOP: The Bold Innovator

LOOP is the newer kid on the block, founded in 2020 by Another Snus Factory AB out of Stockholm. Despite being relatively fresh to the market, LOOP has carved out a distinct identity through aggressive flavor experimentation and a piece of technology they call Instant Rush.

The brand started with tobacco-free, all-white pouches and immediately went for the distinctive angle: unusual flavor blends that you won't find anywhere else. Jalapeño Lime, Habanero Mint, Red Chili Melon, Hot Peach, Hot Mango—these aren't your typical nicotine pouch offerings. They're the kind of thing you try because they sound crazy, then you end up ordering them regularly because they actually work.

LOOP's Instant Rush technology is worth understanding. The promise is immediate flavor and nicotine release paired with a lasting hit. The pouch design and material composition are built to get nicotine into your system fast without sacrificing the experience length—you don't want the buzz to disappear after five minutes.

On the environmental side, LOOP makes a point of using fully recyclable cans (50% plant material, 50% recyclable plastic), which matters if you care about that stuff.

### LOOP's Flavor Range

LOOP currently offers around 30+ products across several flavor families:

- **Spicy lineup:** Jalapeño Lime, Red Chili Melon, Habanero Mint, Spicy Apple
- **Traditional mint:** Smooth Mint, Cool Mint
- **Fruit-forward:** Cassis Bliss (blackcurrant), Hot Peach, Hot Mango
- **Unique blends:** Licorice Fusion, Creamy Cappuccino, Mint Mania

The nicotine content ranges from 6.25mg to 15.5mg per pouch, so there's range here too.

### LOOP's Pouch Feel

LOOP pouches come in slim and mini formats. The material is designed for quick absorption, and users consistently report that the pouches sit comfortably without being too moist or too dry.

## Skruf: The Established Standard

Skruf was founded in 2002 by Jonas Engwall and Adam Gillberg in a small Swedish town called Skruv. It's now one of Sweden's largest smokeless manufacturers and probably the most widely available premium nicotine pouch brand globally. They launched their Super White line in 2018 and haven't looked back.

Skruf's philosophy is different from LOOP's. Rather than chasing bizarre flavors, Skruf focused on getting the fundamentals right: clean materials, consistent quality, and a carefully curated flavor palette. They use microcrystalline cellulose (actual plant fibers), high-purity nicotine, and natural flavorings. The pouches are soft and thin, designed to feel barely noticeable under your lip.

The Super White range was a significant move for Skruf—it positioned them squarely in the tobacco-free, all-white pouch market where the action was moving. Since then, they've become the gold standard for reliability in that category.

### Skruf's Flavor Range

Skruf is deliberately more conservative. You'll find:

- **Mint variations:** Fresh Mint, Frozen Mint, Polar Eucalyptus, Nordic Mint
- **Fruit blends:** Fresh Apple, Aloe Fresh, Indigo Berry (blackcurrant-mint), Cassice (blackcurrant)
- **Specialty:** Crystal Fresh, Purple Cassice, Nordic Licorice

The approach is "elevate the classic" rather than "invent something weird." That's not a criticism—it's intentional positioning.

### Skruf's Strength Levels

Skruf uses a numbered system (#2 through #5) to indicate strength:

- **#2 (Light):** 6mg per pouch—for occasional or new users
- **#3 (Standard):** 10mg per pouch—the workhorse strength
- **#4 (Extra Strong):** 11mg per pouch—for regular users
- **#5 (Ultra Strong):** 16mg per pouch—for experienced users who want a serious hit

The pouches come in slim (0.8g) and super slim (0.7g) formats for maximum discretion.

## Head-to-Head Comparison

### Flavor Range & Variety

**LOOP wins here, hands down.** If you're the type who gets bored or likes to experiment, LOOP gives you permission to try something completely different each time you order. Jalapeño Lime is a genuine flavor experience, not just a marketing angle. You'll either love it or you won't, but you won't be bored.

**Skruf plays it safer.** Their flavors are all good—genuinely well-executed mint and fruit profiles—but they're in the same flavor family as dozens of other brands. The upside is consistency: you know exactly what you're getting, and Skruf pulls off "clean mint" better than almost anyone.

### Strength Options & Nicotine Hit

**LOOP** ranges from 6.25mg to 15.5mg, which covers light to strong users, but the lineup is less clearly organized than Skruf's.

**Skruf** has a tidier strength ladder with clear numbering and genuine range from light to ultra-strong. If you're new to pouches or switching from something stronger, Skruf's #2 and #3 are easier to understand. If you know what you want, both brands deliver.

The Instant Rush tech in LOOP does seem to make a difference in how quickly the nicotine hits, which some users prefer. Skruf is more traditional—good absorption, but not as aggressive.

### Pouch Format & Feel

**LOOP** offers slim and mini formats. The pouches are designed for fast saturation and tend to be slightly more moist. Some people find this ideal; others think it's too wet.

**Skruf** is famous for its soft, thin pouches. The super slim format (0.7g) is genuinely discreet and comfortable. The material is less moist, which appeals to users who prefer a drier experience or want to keep pouches in longer without it becoming unpleasant.

If discretion and comfort are priority one, Skruf's pouch engineering is slightly ahead.

### Price & Value

**Both are reasonably priced.** UK prices range from £3.24 to £3.96 per can depending on retailer. Per-pouch cost is similar between the two when you account for pouch count and strength levels.

**Skruf** might give slightly better value on a per-can basis because the smaller, slimmer pouches use less material. **LOOP** justifies its pricing through flavor innovation and the Instant Rush tech.

### Unique Features

**LOOP's advantage:** Instant Rush technology, experimental flavors, recyclable can design, appeals to users who want something different.

**Skruf's advantage:** Established supply chain (easy to find in most countries), proven consistency, soft pouch engineering, strength labeling that's intuitive, appeals to users who know what they like and want reliable quality.

## Who Should Choose LOOP?

Pick LOOP if:

- You like variety and don't want to get bored with the same mint flavor repeatedly.
- You want a strong, fast nicotine hit and appreciate the Instant Rush technology.
- You're willing to experiment and you like flavor profiles that push boundaries (Habanero Mint, Jalapeño Lime, Hot Mango).
- You want something different from the mainstream nicotine pouch experience.
- You care about sustainable packaging.
- You're an experienced user looking for strength options in that 12–15mg range.

In short: LOOP is for the adventurous user who treats pouches like a hobby and enjoys discovery.

## Who Should Choose Skruf?

Pick Skruf if:

- You know what you want and you want it consistently delivered every time.
- You prefer classic, well-executed flavors—particularly mint—over experimental blends.
- You value comfort and discretion. Skruf's pouches are genuinely thin and soft; they disappear under your lip.
- You're new to nicotine pouches and want an intuitive strength system (#2–#5).
- You want easy global availability. Skruf is stocked in more places.
- You prefer a drier pouch feel that's comfortable for extended periods (1+ hour).

In short: Skruf is for the user who appreciates quality fundamentals and doesn't need to be surprised.

## Final Verdict

There's no wrong choice here. LOOP and Skruf represent two valid approaches to the nicotine pouch category. LOOP is the innovator breaking expectations with unusual flavors and fast-hit technology. Skruf is the reliable professional who's been doing this for years and has refined every detail.

If you're stuck, buy one can of each. LOOP if you want excitement, Skruf if you want consistency. Many serious users actually rotate between them—LOOP for variety, Skruf as the default go-to when you just want something straightforward. The Swedish nicotine pouch industry is strong enough that both can coexist, and honestly, having choices this good is the real win.
