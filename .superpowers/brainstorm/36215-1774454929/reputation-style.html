C: Pill Badges (Enhanced)

Clean pill/tag badges that scale up in visual weight — thicker borders, stronger backgrounds, glow effects at higher tiers. Compact, works inline everywhere. Add CSS animations (pulse for Legend) rather than complex SVGs.

Note: Whichever style we pick, the badge will appear in: leaderboard rows, review cards, community posts, profile header, and as a compact inline version next to usernames. It needs to work at both large (profile card) and small (inline beside name) sizes.
