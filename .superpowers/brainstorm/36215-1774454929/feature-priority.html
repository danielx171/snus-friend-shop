Which 2-3 features should we nail for the CEO demo?

Pick the ones that would be most visually impressive and show the gamification vision best. Select 2 or 3.

1

Reputation System Upgrade

Current: Small colored dot + basic card with progress bar

Potential: Animated tier badges (think gaming rank emblems), glow effects per tier, visible everywhere — leaderboard, reviews, profile. The "status flex" that makes users want to level up.

Effort: Medium | Impact: High (visible on every user interaction)

2

Multi-Step Achievement System

Current: One-off quests (do X once, get points)

Potential: Tiered badges like Xbox achievements — Bronze/Silver/Gold/Diamond for escalating goals (1 review, 5 reviews, 25 reviews, 100 reviews). Trophy case display. Progress chains that keep users coming back.

Effort: Large | Impact: High (core engagement loop)

3

Customizable Avatar System

Current: Avatar gallery with selection, basic attribute editor

Potential: Layered avatar builder (base + accessories + frames + backgrounds). Cosmetic items unlocked via achievements/tiers. Think Bitmoji meets gaming — users build their "snus persona".

Effort: Large | Impact: High (personal identity, shareable)

4

Flavor Quiz Enhancement

Current: 6 questions, radar chart, product recommendations

Potential: Shareable result cards (OG image generation), personality-style naming ("You're a Frost Commander!"), social comparison, re-quiz to track taste evolution. BuzzFeed-quiz viral potential.

Effort: Medium | Impact: Medium-High (viral sharing, onboarding hook)

5

Expert Community Boards

Current: Community post/comment/poll components (not routed to a page)

Potential: Brand ambassador badges, verified expert tags, Q&A threads, expert-curated product picks. Builds trust and drives engagement.

Effort: Large | Impact: Medium (needs active community first)

My recommendation: Reputation + Achievements + Quiz. These three create a complete "status + progression + identity" loop that's visually striking and immediately demo-able. Avatar system is amazing but needs the achievement system first (to drive unlocks). Community needs users first.
