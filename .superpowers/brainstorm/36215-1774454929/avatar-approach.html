Avatar Customization Approach

How deep should the avatar builder go? This determines art requirements and complexity.

A

Avatar Frames + Flair

Keep the current avatar gallery (pick a base image). Add unlockable frames around it — tier-colored borders, animated rings for Legend, seasonal frames (snowflakes, spring flowers). Plus small flair icons (badges, emojis) overlaid on the corner.

Expert frame + level flair

Art needed: Frame SVGs only (5 tiers + seasonal). Low effort.

B

Layered Avatar Builder

Full character builder: pick base shape, face expression, accessory (hat, glasses, headphones), background, and frame. Each layer is a simple SVG. Items unlock via achievements and reputation tier.

C

Pouch Character Builder

Brand-specific twist: your avatar IS a nicotine pouch character. Pick pouch shape, flavor color, expression, hat/accessory, and background scene. Themed to the snus world. Could become the SnusFriend mascot system.

My take: Option A (frames + flair) ships fast and still looks great. Option C (pouch characters) is the most memorable and brandable but needs the most art. Option B is the safe middle ground but lacks personality. For a CEO demo, C would get the biggest reaction — but A is the smartest MVP.
